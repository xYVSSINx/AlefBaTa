Learn, Play & Enjoy Arabic


What do we provide?


Alef Ba Ta teaches Arabic language to children from age 4 - 10 years using joyful and fun interactive lessons.
Children will enjoy learning Arabic without any needed previous knowledge in the language.




Games

Hundreds of educational games that introduces Arabic alphabets, simple words and construction of short sentences.

Sight Words

Introduce top 100 sight words in an engaging way and help the child to easily memorize them

Exercises

Hundreds of educational games that introduces Arabic alphabets, simple words and construction of short sentences.

Stories

Collection of stories arranged in five levels. Every story comes with exercises that focus on language construction & comprehension skills.

Texts

Collection of Arabic texts for older kids, with an objective of enriching the Arabic language and introducing social values.

Grammar

Interactive exercises and printables that focus on teaching Arabic grammar for elementary stage.
